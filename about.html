Who We Are

Animal Haven Rescue is a community-focused animal shelter that provides protection, care, and adoption support for rescued animals. Since 2015, we have helped animals recover in a safe environment while preparing them for loving permanent homes.

What We Offer

  1. Animal rescue and rehabilitation
  2. Vaccination and health support
  3. Volunteer opportunities
  4. Community education

Why Choose Us

  1. Experienced rescue support
  2. Ethical adoption process
  3. Trusted by the community
  4. Focused on animal welfare
